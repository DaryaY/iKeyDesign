Compass Control®

Compass Control® is a fully integrated major control system designed to use iOS and Android devices – as its backbone.

Compass Control® has an entire family of integrated devices allowing plug and play such as: Dbx, Lutron, MechoSystems, Honeywell, Biamp, Leviton, CasaTunes, Somfy, Nest, Ashly, Aprilaire, Dish, Wolfvision, Sonos, Bose, Denon Heos, Autonomic, Russound, Elk, DSC, and many more. Combining the knowledge and expertise from the different CAP brands has allowed Compass Control® to develop into a complete turnkey residential or commercial control solution.

Compass Control replaces all of your remotes and traditional control interfaces with one device. Compass Control becomes your cable remote, TV remote, thermostat, alarm control panel, and many other remote devices all contained within your Android or iOS device.

What it offers:

-FREE Full Featured Demo for Customer Presentations - Great sales tool!

-Instantly integrates third party components to create an entire plug and play system

-Fully programmable with Compass Navigator™

-Replaces all of remotes and traditional control interfaces with one device

-Control your dream system with simple swipe gestures

-Customize and install full App on customer device

-End-user oriented graphic interface
