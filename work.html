Compass Control® iOS and Android App

Compass Control® Modules offer an installer an ever-expanding library of custom built Devices Modules, consisting of a bi-directional driver and a custom User Interface for variety of devices. These modules offer easy integration of deferent devices into any Compass Control® environment.

Compass Control® system supports variety of iOS and Android devices. Device of choice would be a tablet size device used in landscape layout. Tablets offers many options that make the user experience very dynamic, including great graphics engine, the ability to embed images inside other images, quick navigation from page to page, and the ‘flick’ option to choose between items, pages, graphics, etc. All of these features, and more, are available for use when creating the user interface.

As a secondary controller option, the Compass Control® System also supports smaller mobile phones and devices, such as iPhone or Samsung Galaxy phones. These smaller device allows the same level of control as the tablet, but since it has a much smaller footprint, it is recommended that it be used as a secondary remote to the main controller.

Compass Control® App is a FREE Download from Apple App Store or Google Play Stores.

CONTROL SYSTEM DESIGN

Compass Control®

Compass Control® is a fully integrated major control system designed to use iOS and Android devices – as its backbone.

Compass Control® has an entire family of integrated devices allowing plug and play such as: Dbx, Lutron, MechoSystems, Honeywell, Biamp, Leviton, CasaTunes, Somfy, Nest, Ashly, Aprilaire, Dish, Wolfvision, Sonos, Bose, Denon Heos, Autonomic, Russound, Elk, DSC, and many more. Combining the knowledge and expertise from the different CAP brands has allowed Compass Control® to develop into a complete turnkey residential or commercial control solution.

Compass Control replaces all of your remotes and traditional control interfaces with one device. Compass Control becomes your cable remote, TV remote, thermostat, alarm control panel, and many other remote devices all contained within your Android or iOS device.

What it offers:

-FREE Full Featured Demo for Customer Presentations - Great sales tool!

-Instantly integrates third party components to create an entire plug and play system

-Fully programmable with Compass Navigator™

-Replaces all of remotes and traditional control interfaces with one device

-Control your dream system with simple swipe gestures

-Customize and install full App on customer device

-End-user oriented graphic interface
